## STRATEGIC POSITIONING

Agency Group is NOT competing with Compass or Savills on scale.

1. **AI-driven operations** — doing more with fewer people
2. **Off-market access** — courts, banks, servicers (unavailable to traditional agents)
3. **Institutional capital matching** — connecting family offices to off-market deals
4. **Technology moat** — platform that gets better with every deal

**Defensible niche**: Portugal + Spain institutional/off-market deal flow + AI-automated operations
