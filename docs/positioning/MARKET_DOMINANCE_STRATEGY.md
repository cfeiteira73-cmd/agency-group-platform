## Beachhead Strategy: Why Portugal First

1. **Regulatory familiarity:** Portuguese RE law (CPCV, escritura, AMI licensing) is encoded into SH-ROS workflows. Expanding to Spain requires legal re-mapping, not legal re-architecture.

2. **Data density now:** 169,812 transactions in 2026, +17.6% YoY — sufficient training signal for match engine calibration. Smaller markets (Azores: €1,952/m²) train on Portugal base model, not from scratch.

3. **Buyer nationality overlap:** North American, French, British, Chinese, Brazilian buyers active in Portugal are also active in Spain, France Riviera, and increasingly UAE. Portugal buyer intelligence transfers to adjacent markets.

4. **Competitive whitespace:** No well-resourced AI-native competitor in Portuguese luxury RE as of 2026. This window is 18–24 months.

5. **Operational cost:** Building proof in Portugal is cheaper than building proof in London or Paris. CAC is lower. Legal overhead is lower. Iteration cycle is faster.
