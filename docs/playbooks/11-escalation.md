# SOP 11 — Stalled Deal Escalation
**Owner:** Carlos Feiteira | **Trigger:** Deal inactive >7 days in active stage

## Escalation Tiers

| Tier | Trigger | Response Time | Owner | Action |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| L1 — Yellow | No client contact 5 days | 24h | Agent | Urgency call |
| L2 — Orange | No progress 10 days | Same day | Agent + Carlos | Joint strategy |
| L3 — Red | No movement 21 days | <4h | Carlos | Direct intervention |
| L4 — Critical | CPCV at risk | <1h | Carlos | Emergency |

## Stalled Deal Intervention Playbook

### L3 — Carlos Direct Intervention Protocol
1. **Diagnose root cause** (15-min call with agent):
   - Buyer cold feet? Competitor appeared? Financing failed? Legal issue?
2. **Choose intervention type:**
   - Video call with buyer (Carlos presents personally)
   - New property alternative introduced
   - Price renegotiation facilitated
   - Third-party validation (independent AVM)
   - Urgency creation (another buyer, price increase)
3. **Document in CRM:** Add note with diagnosis + action taken
4. **Set 48h deadline:** If no movement → mark as 'stalled' + trigger win/loss recording

### Common Stall Patterns + Solutions

| Pattern | Diagnosis | Intervention |
|---|---|---|
| Buyer stops responding | Cold feet or competitor | Personal WhatsApp from Carlos |
| "We're still thinking" | Decision paralysis | Deadline: "Another buyer viewed this Monday" |
| Financing delayed | Bank processing | Offer bridge financing contact |
| Partner disagrees | Internal conflict | Request joint meeting with both parties |
| New cheaper option | Competitor | Present AVM comparison + exclusive features |
| Legal concern | Due diligence issue | Escalate to advogado immediately |
| Seller suddenly raised price | Mandate violation | Review mandate terms + Carlos advogado call |
| Buyer re-negotiating at CPCV | Bad faith | Document everything, legal route if needed |

## KPIs for Escalated Deals
- Recovery rate (% of L3 deals that convert)
- Average days to recover
- Commission at risk in L3+ pipeline
- Carlos intervention conversion rate

## L4 — CPCV at Risk Emergency Protocol

Triggered when: deposit not received by due date, CPCV challenged legally, or buyer/seller defaults.

**First 1 hour:**
1. Carlos alerted (phone call, not just WhatsApp)
2. Advogado alerted
3. Situation assessed: intentional default or process issue?
4. All communication paused until legal review

**Options matrix:**
- Process issue (banking delay): 24h grace + formal written extension
- Buyer default: execute penalty clause (seller retains deposit)
- Seller default: demand return of 2× deposit + potential legal claim
- Force majeure: negotiate mutually agreed exit

**Documentation required:**
- All communications (WhatsApp + email) screenshotted and filed
- Timeline of events written up
- No verbal agreements — everything in writing from L4 onward
