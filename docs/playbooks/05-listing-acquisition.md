# SOP 05 — Listing Acquisition
**Owner:** Agent | **SLA:** Pitch delivered within 48h of first contact with seller | **Platform:** `/portal/crm`

## Overview
Listing acquisition is the foundation of inventory. Every new mandate requires a structured seller qualification, professional valuation, and mandate negotiation. The goal is exclusive mandates at market price with a 90–180 day window.

---

## Phase 1 — Seller Qualification

### Initial Contact Assessment
Before investing time in a valuation, qualify the seller:

| Question | Why It Matters |
|---|---|
| Why are you selling? | Urgency level, motivation, flexibility |
| What is your target price? | Gap to market, negotiability |
| What is your ideal timeline? | 30 days? 6 months? No rush? |
| Have other agencies been approached? | Exclusivity feasibility |
| Is the property mortgaged? | Complexity, net proceeds calc |
| Who else is involved in the decision? | Authority — spouse, partners, heirs |

### Seller Motivation Matrix

| Motivation | Urgency | Approach |
|---|---|---|
| Financial need (divorce, debt) | HIGH | Move fast, price realistically |
| Upgrade/downgrade | MEDIUM | Education on market, time to align |
| Investment realization | MEDIUM | ROI-focused pitch |
| Inheritance / estate | LOW-MEDIUM | Build trust, involve family |
| Testing the market | LOW | Set expectations, secure exclusivity |
| Relocation / emigration | HIGH | Speed as primary driver |

**Decision gate:** Is the seller motivated and realistic within 20% of market value?
- YES → Proceed to valuation
- NO (>20% above market, no real intent) → Log as prospect, revisit in 90 days

## Phase 2 — Property Valuation

### AVM (Automated Valuation Model) — Portal Tool
1. Navigate to `/portal` → AVM section
2. Input property details: zone, m², tipologia, year, condition, parking, extras
3. Review the AVM output: estimated value, confidence interval, comparable sales
4. Export AVM report (PDF) for presentation to seller

### Comparative Market Analysis (CMA)
Supplement the AVM with manual CMA:
1. Find 5 comparable active listings in same zone, same typology, ±20% m²
2. Find 3 recent sales (<6 months) in same zone
3. Adjust for: floor level, views, condition, parking, amenities
4. Calculate price per m² range: low / mid / high scenario

### Valuation Output Document
Prepare a 1-page summary including:
- AVM estimate + confidence band
- Comparable analysis: €X/m² range
- Recommended listing price (mid scenario)
- Aggressive listing price (high, if >60 days acceptable)
- Minimum acceptable price (low, for fast sale)
- Estimated selling timeline at each price point

**Reference prices (2026):**
- Lisboa: €5.000/m² avg | Premium zones: €7.000+
- Cascais: €4.713/m²
- Algarve: €3.941/m²
- Porto: €3.643/m²
- Madeira: €3.760/m²
- Açores: €1.952/m²

## Phase 3 — Mandate Negotiation

### Pitch Meeting Preparation
- Bring printed AVM + CMA report
- Prepare Agency Group credentials (portfolio, past sales, testimonials)
- Prepare marketing plan (portals, social, international network, Sofia AI)
- Know your walk-away: minimum mandate terms you will accept

### Pitch Structure (60 min meeting)

**1. Discovery (15 min)**
- Let seller talk: situation, history, expectations
- Active listening — do not pitch prematurely

**2. Valuation Presentation (20 min)**
- Present AVM + CMA professionally
- Show 3 scenarios: fast sale / market price / premium attempt
- Anchor the realistic price range

**3. Agency Group Value Proposition (15 min)**
- International buyer network (Americans 16%, French 13%, British 9%)
- Portal with Sofia AI and digital marketing
- AVM tools, photography, virtual tours
- Legal support coordination
- 5% commission — justify with ROI, not defense

**4. Mandate Proposal (10 min)**
- Propose exclusive mandate
- Duration: 90–180 days recommended
- Commission: 5% (payable at Escritura)
- Price: agreed listing price + revision clause if no offers in 60 days

### Exclusive vs. Shared Mandate

| Factor | Exclusive | Shared |
|---|---|---|
| Marketing investment | Full | Limited |
| Negotiation control | Agent | Chaotic |
| Price discipline | Maintained | Often undercut |
| Commitment to seller | Total | Partial |
| Our preference | ALWAYS | Last resort |

**Script — Handling "I want multiple agencies":**
*"Compreendo. Muitos vendedores pensam que mais agências = mais exposição. Na realidade, o que acontece é: preços inconsistentes que confundem compradores, agências que vendem rápido pelo preço errado, e menos investimento de cada agência porque não há garantia de comissão. Com mandato exclusivo, invisto €X em marketing garantido e a minha atenção 100%."*

### Mandate Agreement Essentials
The mandate document must include:
- [ ] Property description and address
- [ ] Agreed listing price
- [ ] Commission: 5% + IVA (payable at Escritura)
- [ ] Mandate duration (recommend 180 days, minimum 90)
- [ ] Exclusivity clause (or shared — with terms)
- [ ] Price revision clause (if no qualified offer in 60 days → revisit price together)
- [ ] Agency Group AMI 22506 identification
- [ ] GDPR clause covering data processing
- [ ] Seller's identification (NIF + document)
- [ ] Authorized signatory confirmation

## Phase 4 — Listing Preparation

### Photography & Staging Requirements

**Photography Standards:**
- Professional photographer only (no mobile phone photos)
- Minimum 20 photos per property
- Exterior: front, rear, garden/terrace, parking
- Interior: all rooms, kitchen, bathrooms
- Views and surroundings
- Twilight/dusk shot for premium properties (>€800K)

**Staging Checklist (before photography):**
- [ ] All personal items removed or minimized
- [ ] Surfaces clean and decluttered
- [ ] All lights working and switched on
- [ ] Curtains/blinds open (maximize light)
- [ ] Garden/terrace cleaned and presentable
- [ ] Any visible defects addressed or disclosed
- [ ] Air freshened (ventilate 30 min before visit/shoot)

**Virtual Tour:**
- Required for properties >€500K
- Matterport 3D or equivalent
- Upload to property listing before publishing

### Portal Upload Checklist
Before marking a listing as `active` in the portal:

**Required fields:**
- [ ] Title (PT + EN minimum)
- [ ] Full description (PT + EN minimum, min 300 words each)
- [ ] Price set correctly (matches mandate)
- [ ] All photos uploaded (min 20, max 50)
- [ ] Virtual tour link (if >€500K)
- [ ] Floor plan uploaded
- [ ] Zona + sub-zona tagged
- [ ] Tipologia set
- [ ] Area (m² útil + bruto)
- [ ] Year of construction
- [ ] Condition (renovated/good/to renovate)
- [ ] Parking (yes/no + spaces)
- [ ] Energy certificate class
- [ ] Condominium fees (if applicable)
- [ ] IMI value (annual)
- [ ] Features checklist completed (pool, garden, terrace, etc.)
- [ ] Reference number generated
- [ ] Published to external portals: Idealista, Imovirtual, Rightmove, Kyero

**SEO fields:**
- [ ] Meta title (max 60 chars)
- [ ] Meta description (max 155 chars)
- [ ] Alt text on all photos

## Phase 5 — Pricing Strategy Framework

### Pricing Scenarios

| Scenario | Markup over AVM | Timeline Target | Use When |
|---|---|---|---|
| Aggressive (fast sale) | -5% to -10% | <60 days | Seller needs liquidity |
| Market (optimal) | ±0% to +5% | 60–120 days | Standard motivation |
| Premium attempt | +5% to +15% | 90–180 days | Unique property, flexible seller |
| Overpriced (not recommended) | >15% | Unknown | Refuse or set strict conditions |

### Price Adjustment Protocol
If no qualified showing in 30 days → recommend 3–5% price reduction.
If no offer in 60 days → mandatory pricing review meeting with seller.
If on market >120 days → full reassessment: AVM refresh, competition analysis, price correction.

**Rule:** Never let a listing sit >180 days without a price event or withdrawal. Stale listings damage brand perception.
