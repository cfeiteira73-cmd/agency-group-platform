# SOP 08 — Negotiation Playbook
**Owner:** Agent (lead) + Carlos Feiteira (strategic oversight) | **Threshold:** Deals >€1M

## Agency Group Negotiation Framework

### Core Principles
1. **Never negotiate against yourself.** First offer stands until counter.
2. **Always anchor high on seller side, conservative on buyer side.**
3. **Every concession must extract a concession.** Quid pro quo.
4. **Timeline creates urgency.** Use CPCV deadline as leverage.
5. **Protect the commission.** Never reduce fee without explicit authorization from Carlos.

## Negotiation Phases

### Phase 1 — Position Establishment
- Obtain written offer (even informal)
- Verify buyer proof of funds / financing approval
- Check seller's urgency level and reserve price (confidential)
- Establish our walk-away points BEFORE engaging

### Phase 2 — Counter Framework
| Spread from Asking | Strategy |
|---|---|
| <3% | Accept or counter at asking — don't over-negotiate |
| 3–8% | Counter at 2% below asking, justify with market data |
| 8–15% | Counter at 5% below asking, request AVM report first |
| >15% | Request buyer proof of funds, validate intent, counter at 7% |

### Phase 3 — Deadlock Resolution
Options in order:
1. Additional value (furniture, parking, storage included)
2. Payment structure (larger CPCV, faster escritura)
3. Escrow / third-party validation
4. Time extension with price lock
5. Carlos intervention (in-person or video call)

### Escalation Triggers → Immediate Carlos Involvement
- Spread >20% of asking price
- Seller threatens to pull from market
- Buyer brings own legal counsel
- Commission reduction request
- Counter-offer with conditions (financing contingency, inspection waiver)

## Script — Price Objection (PT)
*"Compreendo que o preço é um fator crítico. Vamos analisar juntos: o valor por m² deste imóvel é €X, vs €Y de média nesta zona. O vendedor tem margem de X%, mas temos de demonstrar seriedade. Uma proposta com CPCV em 30 dias e escritura em 90 vai ser recebida muito melhor do que uma proposta com preço mais baixo mas condições incertas."*

## Script — Price Objection (EN)
*"I completely understand price is key. Let me show you the context: this property is at €X/m² versus the zone average of €Y/m². The seller has some flexibility, but the strongest offer is one that shows conviction — a solid CPCV deposit with a clear timeline matters as much as the headline number."*

## Script — Urgency Creation
*"Just so you're aware, we had another viewing on this property last Tuesday, and I know there's interest from at least one other party. I don't know their timeline, but I'd hate for you to miss this because of a small price gap. Can we agree on [price] today and move this forward?"*

## Concession Management
Track all concessions in deal notes:
- What was conceded
- What was extracted in return
- Who made the move
- Date and format (verbal or written)

**Concession sequence:**
1. Furniture / equipment included (easy win, low cost)
2. Extended payment of agent fee (buyer or seller flexibility)
3. Faster CPCV timeline (shows commitment)
4. Price movement — only after above exhausted
5. Price below reserve → STOP, escalate to Carlos

## Commission Defense
If buyer/seller requests fee reduction:
1. Reframe: "O meu trabalho pagou-se X vezes nesta negociação."
2. Quantify: present time invested, contacts made, viewings organized
3. Escalate to Carlos — NEVER agree to reduction without his approval
4. Last resort: structured deal (flat fee for specific services)

## Multi-Offer Situations
When seller has multiple offers:
1. Inform all buyers simultaneously that best and final is required
2. Set 48h deadline for best and final
3. Advise seller on evaluation criteria: price, certainty (cash vs. mortgage), timeline
4. Never reveal competing offer amounts to any buyer
5. Document entire process in CRM notes

## Post-Negotiation
- Document all offers and counters in deal notes
- Record outcome in win_loss_events (CRM)
- If lost: conduct debrief within 48h, log objection type
- If won: trigger SOP 09 Contract & Legal immediately
