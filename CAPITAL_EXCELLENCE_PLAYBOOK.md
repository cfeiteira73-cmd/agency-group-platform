## CONVERSATION STRATEGY (by persona)

### FAMILY_OFFICE
**What they want:** Discretion, exclusivity, strong due diligence, off-market access  
**Pain:** Public listings are overpriced, over-competed. They want off-market.  
**Angle:** "We have off-market opportunities not available anywhere else."  
**First message:** "We have a unique off-market asset in [Zone] at €[Price]. Our FO clients have been asking for this type of opportunity. Discretion assured."

### REAL_ESTATE_FUND
**What they want:** Yield data, market comps, portfolio fit, scale  
**Pain:** Need multiple assets, need data, need exit strategy  
**Angle:** "We can structure a deal pack with AVM, yield analysis, and comparable transactions."  
**First message:** "Given your fund's allocation to Southern European RE, I wanted to share our latest market intelligence on Lisbon Prime — cap rates, transaction volumes, and 3 specific opportunities."

### WEALTH_MANAGER
**What they want:** Client solutions, easy process, credibility  
**Pain:** Clients ask about Portugal; WM needs trusted local partner  
**Angle:** "We are the institutional local partner for your Portugal-interested HNW clients."  
**First message:** "Do any of your clients have interest in Portuguese premium real estate? We handle the entire process for HNW clients (sourcing, due diligence, legal coordination, key handover)."

### INVESTOR
**What they want:** ROI, specific opportunity, clear numbers  
**Angle:** "Lisbon prime: €5,000/m². Our AVM shows 4.2% gross yield on €1.2M investment."  
**First message:** Market data first, then ask about interest.
